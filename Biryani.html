Haleem

This recipe is my favourite rice dish. It's a curry that gets absorbed by the white basmati rice, with chicken.

It's best eaten with chutney and salad.

Ingredients:

  • White Basmati Rice
  • Chicken
  • Biryani mix
  • Salad Veggies
  • Yoghurt

Steps:

  1. Wash and drain rice
  2. Prep Chicken
  3. Combine using some magicks
  4. Add drizzle of spices
  5. Serve and eat with yoghurt
