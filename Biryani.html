Biryani

This recipe is my favourite of all time. It's a lentil based soup, that also contains rice and chicken.

It's best eaten with naan bread.

Ingredient:

  • Lentils
  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Lime
  • Naan

Steps:

  1. Wash and drain lentils
  2. Blend rice
  3. Combine using some magicks
  4. Add drizzle of lime
  5. Serve and eat with naan
